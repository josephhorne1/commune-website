The Pillow Bag

A soft object shaped around the body, developed to sit somewhere between utility, companion, and personal symbol.

01 / Origin

Designed to become part of its owner's being.

The project began in late spring 2022 with a simple proposition: make a bag elemental enough to feel inevitable, yet distinct enough to carry the values of a new independent label.

Its oversized padded body resists the hard geometry of conventional carry goods. The central opening becomes handle, frame, and gesture; the volume shifts as the bag meets the wearer.

02 / Campaign

A body holding another body.

03 / Object system

One opening. Infinite posture.

Repeated digital development tested the bag as profile, crossbody, shoulder object, and wearable volume before campaign production translated that behaviour into image.

04 / First object sold

MT570 will be refunding the first bag sold.

The launch language treated the first sale as a community marker rather than a transaction. A product story, identity gesture, and thank-you were compressed into one direct campaign object.

570 / Ongoing language

The bag established more than a product. It opened an identity system for future garments, symbols, colour families, and stories—an adaptable world built from softness and persistence.
