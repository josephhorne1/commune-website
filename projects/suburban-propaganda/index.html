01 / The collection

The suburb promises order. The image interrupts it.

Suburban Propaganda treats clothing as a carrier signal. Protective shells, exposed limbs, anonymous heads, and domestic colours move between comfort and control.

03 / Graphic field

The flag is a body. The body is a flag.

Hand-built symbols combine devotional image, starburst, target, and broadcast artifact. They work as atmosphere rather than explanation: repeated until they begin to feel official.

A collection staged as evidence.

The visual system moves from isolated garment studies to accumulated contact sheets, degraded fields, and synthetic landscapes. The archive is part lookbook, part recovered document.
